That's me

Hey, I am Manish Patil a UX UI Specialist with over 2.5 years of experience and currently associated with Amdocs India and previously at iMocha and Winvesta. Over the years I've helped organisations conceptualise, design and publish their apps on prominent app stores and enhanced experiences.

I discovered the field of design for mobile and computer through my enthusiasm for technology along with the habit of exploring creative applications and tools over the internet. The fun part about this is that, I used to download trial versions, explore them extensively and learn new design terminologies and the underlying fields in parallel.

Design to me has always been about creating pleasing screens and at the same time solving the user problems and meeting the business goals, and with a strong background in Business and IT, I am always up for a challenge. Furthermore, I love building delightful experiences, especially mobile products. They're simply fun!

My favourite go to tools while designing and exploring designs are figma, unsplash, behance and dribbble.
